Widget Unit cost: 12,000.56 dollars
Taxes: 234.00 dollars
Total: 12,234.56 dollars

pattern: r"(?P<value>\d+(,\d{3})*(\.\d{2})?)\s+dollar(s)?"

explanations:

(?P<value>...) = name group
\d+ = multiple digits
(,\d{3})* = multiple 3 digits separated by comma
(\.\d{2})? = possible one set of 2 digits representing cents (which starts with a demical) and ? = possible
\s+ = one or more space
dollar(s) = follow the word dollar and possible with s at the end
